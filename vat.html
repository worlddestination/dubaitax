When to Register for VAT
Mandatory when annual taxable turnover exceeds AED 375,000. Voluntary registration allowed from AED 187,500.
🔖
Zero-Rated Categories
Exports outside UAE, international transport, certain food items, healthcare services, and educational services qualify for 0% VAT.

VAT Filing Deadlines
Monthly filers: 28th of next month. Quarterly filers: 28th after quarter end. Late filing attracts FTA penalties.
